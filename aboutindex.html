About Muse Creative

A little bit about Me...

Hi! I'm Helena, thank you for taking the time to check out my page! I am a young graphic designer and artist, living and working in the Comox Valley. I do work either locally, or remote to suit your needs. I enjoy spending time in nature, and get inspiration from the people and places around me. Originally i grew up in Northern Alberta, but moved to Quadra Island, BC as a teenager. I have always loved art and music, and now spend my time enveloping myself in my passions as much as i can. I strive to create meaningful, beautiful peices that will exceed your expectations and keep you coming back for more.
